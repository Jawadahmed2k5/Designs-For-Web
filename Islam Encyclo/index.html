Our Features

Islam Encyclo is a comprehensive Islamic lifestyle app that features the Holy Quran, Hadiths, accurate Prayer times and more.

Read Quran

Islam Encyclo offers a seamless and immersive Quran reading experience, allowing you to access the Holy Quran anytime, anywhere. With a beautifully designed interface, clear Arabic text, and smooth navigation, you can read the Quran with ease. Whether you're looking for specific Surahs, Juz, or translations, our app provides a user-friendly experience to enhance your spiritual journey."

Audio Quran

Islam Encyclo not only allows you to read the Holy Quran but also provides an immersive Audio Quran feature. You can listen to high-quality recitations by renowned Qaris, making it easier to learn, understand, and reflect on the verses. Whether you're on the go or prefer listening to the Quran, our app ensures a seamless and spiritually enriching experience.
